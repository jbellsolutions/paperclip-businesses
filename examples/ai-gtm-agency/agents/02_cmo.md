**Post 2: The $500K Marketing Mistake**
"I see it constantly: Series A startups hiring a $150K marketing agency for a $2M ARR company.

The agency is great. But your problem isn't marketing quality—it's that you haven't defined your ICP. You haven't nailed your messaging. You don't have a repeatable sales process.

First: nail your message. Then: scale the campaigns."

**Post 3: Cold Email Myths That Cost You Deals**
"Myth 1: Cold email is dead.
Reality: Cold email works IF you have message-market fit. Most startup emails fail because they're generic, not because cold email doesn't work.

Myth 2: You need a huge list.
Reality: 100 targeted emails to your exact ICP will outperform 10,000 generic emails every time.

Myth 3: Response rates of 5% are normal.
Reality: If you're getting 5%, your targeting or messaging is broken. Top performers hit 15-25%.

A/B test your subject lines. Personalize. Test timing. Measure relentlessly."
